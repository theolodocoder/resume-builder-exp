## ATS (Applicant Tracking System) Compatibility

All templates are **optimized for ATS**, which means:

‚úÖ **What ATS Systems Read:**
- Plain text content
- Proper HTML structure
- Standard fonts
- Simple formatting
- Clear section headers

‚ùå **What ATS Systems Ignore:**
- Images and graphics
- Complex CSS styling
- Unusual colors
- Fancy fonts not installed on recruiter's system
- Text in non-standard formats

### Why ATS Score Matters

ATS software is used by 99% of Fortune 500 companies and most medium-to-large organizations. A high ATS score ensures:
- Your resume is parsed correctly
- All content is readable by machines
- Keyword matching works properly
- Formatting doesn't cause content loss

## PDF vs DOCX Output

### PDF Download
- **Preserves:** Exact visual design
- **Fonts:** Embedded in file
- **Best for:** Submitting online, guarantees appearance
- **Opens in:** All PDF readers (universal)

### DOCX Download
- **Preserves:** Content and basic formatting
- **Fonts:** Based on system fonts available
- **Best for:** Further editing, sharing with hiring managers
- **Opens in:** Microsoft Word, Google Docs, etc.

### Recommendation
- **PDF for:** Applications where exact appearance matters
- **DOCX for:** Situations where editing might be needed

## Common Questions

### Q: Which template should I use?
**A:** Start with Calibri Clean for best ATS compatibility, or Professional if you want more visual appeal.

### Q: Can I switch templates after starting?
**A:** Yes! All your data is preserved. Switch anytime to preview different looks.

### Q: Will changing templates affect my content?
**A:** No. Changing templates only changes the visual style, not your content.

### Q: Do different templates affect the PDF size?
**A:** Slightly. Professional and Lora are slightly larger (embedded fonts). Others use system fonts.

### Q: Can ATS systems read all these templates?
**A:** Yes! All templates are specifically optimized for ATS compatibility.

### Q: Which template looks best when printed?
**A:** Professional and Garamond Classic look excellent in print due to serif fonts.

### Q: Which template is most mobile-friendly?
**A:** Calibri Clean and Compact Executive due to simpler styling.
